Welcome to the Maracaibo Chamber of Commerce

This website serves as a resource hub for businesses in Maracaibo, Venezuela. We provide information on local events, networking opportunities, business directories, and encourage local shopping. Join us in promoting the economic growth of Maracaibo and supporting local businesses.

Our Goals

  • Improve member engagement by offering valuable resources and networking opportunities.
  • Attract new businesses and promote economic development in Maracaibo.
  • Enhance the visibility and reputation of the chamber as a trusted local business authority.

Target Market

We serve business owners, new residents, and patrons in Maracaibo and surrounding areas, providing essential resources for local businesses to thrive.

User Personas

  • Small Business Owner: Maria, a 35-year-old entrepreneur who owns a boutique in Maracaibo, seeks networking opportunities and resources to grow her business.
  • Corporate Executive: Diego, a 47-year-old executive, is looking for partnership opportunities and economic development resources to expand his company's presence in Maracaibo.
  • New Resident: Francisco, who recently moved to Maracaibo, is exploring local services and is interested in the business directories, events, and community service opportunities available.
